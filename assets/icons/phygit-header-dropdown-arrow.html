What is Phygital?

Physical + Digital = Phygital.Combination of two worlds in one product.
Next stage of product development.New standard of modern business.

How it Works

We connect physical items with personal web-apps loaded with engagement user experiences and digital marketing tools via NFC tags or QR-codes.
Without app installation!

Phygital CMS

Phygitalization Benefits

You can combine several features in one product

  • Make your customers go WOW by adding branded web apps into existing products

  • Increase profitability by combining physical products and digital features

  • Talk to your customer directly from your product via integrated web apps

  • Attract new generations of audiences with brand-new emotional engagement

The great thing about Phygit is that you may not just add value to existing products, but you create new ones.You can even start your own business with a new phygital product. All you need to do is to think of a physical asset you want to sell and what digital experience you want it to have.
