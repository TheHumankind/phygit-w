Empower your product via digital personalization

Easy Like 1-2-3

No-code, no-app, and low overhead
  • Step 1. Brand adds NFC tag or QR on the apparel;
  • Step 2. User taps NFC tag or scans QR with the phone;
  • Step 3. Phygit delivers digital experience to the user's screen.

What is it for?

  • a digital asset can be seamlessly integrated into any design;
  • customer engagement-oriented funnel via gamification and exclusive content for customer retention and loyalty;
  • unique user flow to convert NFC scans into emails and personas for further targeting, email marketing, and customer analytics;
  • recommended products and upsells;
  • easy way to keep up communication and interaction with the audience: it can even be the key to the audience gathering point.
