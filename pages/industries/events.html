Events

Enhance your event experience with digital personalization

Easy Like 1-2-3

No-code, no-app, and low overhead
  • Step 1. Brand adds NFC tag or QR on the apparel;
  • Step 2. User taps NFC tag or scans QR with his phone;
  • Step 3. Phygit delivers digital experience to the user's screen.

What is it for?

  • attendee engagement-oriented funnel with gamification and exclusive content;
  • welcome packs for participants with WOW effect;
  • an instrument for intra-event networking acceleration;
  • giving your participants a sense of being a part of a particular community;
  • easy way to maintain communication strategy and interaction with the audience after the ending of the event;
  • digitally personalized gifts would encourage more favourable attitude.

Why Phygit?

  • mobile-first web application focused on the customer UX;
