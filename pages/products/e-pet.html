E-Pet 🐾

How it Works

E-Pets comes as a durable, high-quality tag with NFC technology and a QR code, allowing easy scanning, and transfer of information.
Compatible with iPhone and Android phones.
Without app installation!

E-Pets Features

Lost Pet

In this mode, the contact information of the owner is shown. When scanning the NFC/QR code, the owner's email will receive information about time and approximate location.

Exhibition

A mode that includes displaying additional information such as your pet's awards, pedigree, and the ability to exchange contacts with the owner.
