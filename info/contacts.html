Our mission

The idea behind Phygit is to make it possible for the economy, even traditional businesses, to grow without harming the environment and turning physical resources into "trash". Our solution makes that possible. The same physical products can be sold at a higher price: they will provide value longer and have their own history. Be personal to the Customer and more profitable for brands. And all of this digitally and sustainably.
  • Passion
    Customer's value, innovation, and emotions from the product. To develop a solution that will make billions of people happy.
  • Values
    Proactivity, responsibility, result-oriented, social and environmental responsibility.
  • Initial Idea
    Create new brand-new value by disrupting the gap between digital and physical.
  • Vision
    To be one of the world's best platforms for:
    ● connecting Brand and end-Customer via phygital products;
    ● creating Digital Twin of the Customer based on big data;
    ● engaging Customers through hyper-personalized experience.
